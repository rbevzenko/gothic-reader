What is it

Gothic script made readable

Fraktur Reader uses AI to recognize and modernize texts printed in Gothic typefaces. Upload a PDF or paste a link — get a clean, readable translation in seconds.

For whom

Built for researchers and legal professionals

Anyone working with 19th-century German sources will find Fraktur Reader indispensable.

How it works

Three ways to use it

1

Upload a PDF

Upload a scanned book or document — the app processes each page automatically

2

Paste a link

Copy a page URL from the Max Planck Institute library — the app fetches and translates it instantly

3

Upload a screenshot

Take a photo or screenshot of any Gothic text and upload it — works with printed and handwritten sources
